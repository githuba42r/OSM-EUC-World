### How It Works

1. **Install EUC World**: This is a companion app - you need EUC World installed and running on your device
2. **Enable the Plugin**: The app automatically connects to EUC World's internal API
3. **Use with OsmAnd**: Launch OsmAnd and see your EUC data in custom widgets
4. **Android Auto**: Connect your phone to your car to view EUC stats on your car's display
5. **Track Your Rides**: Use the three independent trip meters to track different segments
