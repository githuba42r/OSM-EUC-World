# Privacy Policy for EUC OsmAnd Plugin

**Last Updated: February 19, 2026**

## Overview

EUC OsmAnd Plugin ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

## Information Collection and Use

**The App does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data.**

The App is designed to integrate with OsmAnd to provide Electric Unicycle (EUC) data display functionality. All data processing occurs locally on your device.

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Device information
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports
- Any other personal or sensitive user data

## Third-Party Services

The App integrates with OsmAnd, a third-party mapping application. When using OsmAnd features, you are subject to OsmAnd's own privacy policy. Please review their privacy policy separately.

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no user data that could be compromised.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, the App is safe for use by all age groups.
